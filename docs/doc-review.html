2  Document review

Decisions about which, when, and how parcels are developed in every locality are guided by a collection of long-range plans and specific regulations. This section outlines the policy and regulatory texts used in the analysis.

2.1 Rockbridge County

County of Rockbridge Comprehensive Plan 2016-2036 (PDF)
Adopted November 2016

County of Rockbridge Land Development Regulations (PDF)
Adopted 1982, amended through March 2023

These regulations include the zoning and subdivision ordinances in a single document.

  • Zoning district uses and use regulations are covered in Articles 6 and 7.
  • The process and requirements for subdivision of land are covered in Article 9.

County of Rockbridge Short-Term Rental Zoning Regulations (PDF)
Adopted October 2021
